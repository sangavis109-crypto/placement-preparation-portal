Interview Questions

Practice common HR and Technical Interview Questions 🚀

1. Tell me about yourself

Introduce yourself briefly including your education and skills.

2. What are your strengths?

Mention your skills, confidence, teamwork, or problem-solving.

3. Why should we hire you?

Explain your skills and willingness to learn.

4. What are your weaknesses?

Mention a weakness and explain how you are improving it.

5. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?

Talk about your career goals and growth.

6. Why do you want to join our company?

Explain what attracts you to the company and role.

7. What is teamwork?

Explain how people work together to achieve a common goal.

8. Tell me about a challenge you faced.

Describe a problem and how you solved it.

9. What are your technical skills?

Mention programming languages, tools and technologies you know.

10. Do you have any questions for us?

Ask about the role, team or growth opportunities.
